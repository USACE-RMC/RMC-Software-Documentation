# Preface

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) Risk Management Center (RMC), in collaboration with the Engineer Research and Development Center (ERDC)
Coastal and Hydraulics Laboratory (CHL), developed the Bayesian estimation and fitting software (RMC-BestFit) to enhance and expedite flood hazard
assessments within the Flood Risk Management, Planning, and Dam and Levee Safety communities of practice.
