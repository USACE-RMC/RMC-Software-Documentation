# Risk Analysis

The Risk Analysis module stores computed risk results that combine failure probabilities from PFM modules with consequence estimates to produce annual probability of failure (APF), annualized life loss, and other risk metrics.

All tables in this chapter are planned and not yet implemented in the database.

## Relationship to Other Modules

Risk analysis draws inputs from two sources:

- **PFM modules** — Active SRP curves from each PFM's `ActiveSrp` table provide failure probabilities at each hazard level.
- **Consequence module** — Life safety and economic consequence estimates are combined with failure probabilities to compute annualized risk metrics.

The total APF is computed by integrating failure probabilities from all evaluated PFMs with the hazard curve to produce an annual probability of failure.
